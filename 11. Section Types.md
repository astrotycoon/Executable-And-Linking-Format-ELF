A section header's `sh_type` member specifies the section's semantics.

### SHT_NULL

This value marks the section header as inactive; it does not have an associated section. Other members of the section header have undefined values.

当节头表表项成员`sh_type`类型为**SHT_NULL**时，表明该节头表表项是无效的（非活动的），目标文件中没有它对应的节，其他成员的值是未定义的（例外的是，当该节头表表项是第0个表项时，其`sh_size`/`sh_link`/`sh_info`有可能尤其特殊的含义，具体详见[``Extended Section Header"](https://github.com/astrotycoon/Executable-And-Linking-Format-ELF/blob/main/15.%20Extended%20Section%20Header.md)）。
